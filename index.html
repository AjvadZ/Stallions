About Us //

At Stallions International, we manufacture one of the best equestrian products in the world. We are the same Master Saddlers who bring to you the world-class Leather Goods from Eastern Saddlery and the significantly recognized Leather Art.

At Stallions International, you'll find products of unparalleled quality, built on a foundation of premium raw materials and master craftsmanship. Our dedication to excellence is evident in our carefully managed production environment and comprehensive quality control, guaranteeing products that consistently meet international benchmarks. We deeply value the partnership between a rider and their horse, which is why our designs uniquely focus on the comfort and performance benefits for both.
